Welcome to AuthorSight!

AuthorSight aims to be the one-stop solution to help you achieve your goals of sharing your knowledge with the world and helping tech communities learn and grow.

With AuthorSight, you can say goodbye to having to deal with multiple tools to help you achieve what you've set out to do. You will be able to input and manage your personal, professional and banking information, whilst also being able to obtain insights around the products you're associated with. Having a zoomed out view of your on-going projects gives you the ability to prioritize tasks and plan your work better, while keeping continuous track of how your published book is performing, helps in planning and executing promotional activities to make sure your efforts reach the maximum audience.

Currently in Beta, AuthorSight will be delivered iteratively with new and exciting features being added with every release. We hope you find it useful and request you to make use of the in-page popup surveys to tell us what you like and what you think could be improved.
